WELCOME TO Cheer Cupcakes!

We combine patisserie art at star level with an extra portion of passion and a lot of attention to detail. We are infatuated with sweets,we love eggs and butter and sugar and cream. We create little works of art from them to our heart's content and according to individual preferences,which taste as good as they look. We like to reinterpret, because what's good can always be better.

WE ARE Cheer!

We are Reem and Mohammed. We learned our trade in star gastronomy, but our passion for sweets is innate. We are convinced and never tire of giving shape and taste to our ideas. Everything can, nothing must and so in our small creative bakery constantly new creations and modern interpretations of sweet classics. Always in small form, always cleverly composed and decorated with dedication.

Enjoy Our Freshy Cupcakes with coffee

We are committed to using fresh, high end, local ingredients and proudly serve locally roasted Joe Van Gogh coffee and espresso. The shop boasts a beautiful, light filled cafe where you can enjoy a croissant warm from the oven with your espresso, or you can take your treats to our patio and enjoy the outdoors with your dessert both day and night.
